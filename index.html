Character progression

Life Paths turn actions into reputation.

Life Paths give characters themed objectives, XP and unlocks. Early progression starts at street level before deeper specialist paths become available.

Daily & long-term

How progression works

  • Choose or begin the available starter route.
  • Complete daily and milestone objectives.
  • Earn path XP from real gameplay actions.
  • Unlock perks, specialist tasks and stronger opportunities.
  • Build a character identity through the work you actually do.

Examples across the city

  • Bins, recycling and street survival.
  • Mechanic diagnostics, repairs and road calls.
  • Deliveries, businesses and supplier progression.
  • Creature surveys, collecting and care.
  • Future legal, criminal and specialist paths.
Daily objectives are paced. Once a daily task is completed, move on to another activity instead of repeating one action endlessly.

Apartments & storage

Your backpack travels. Your home keeps the rest safe.

Portable storage and apartment storage have different jobs. Learning that difference early keeps your inventory clean and stops important items being carried everywhere.

Keep organised

Backpack

  • Use it for items you need while travelling.
  • Keep job tools and everyday supplies organised.
  • Different backpack types may offer different storage value.
  • Avoid filling portable space with items that belong at home.

Apartment tabs

  • Storage keeps spare items and long-term supplies at home.
  • Wardrobe access handles saved outfits and character clothing.
  • Home controls give you a safe base before entering city life.
  • Future properties can expand how your character lives and stores belongings.
Simple rule: carry what your current scene needs and store the rest. This makes jobs, searches and inventory management much easier.

City standards

Serious roleplay
without the confusion.

The final detailed rulebook can be linked from Discord. The website gives visitors a clear overview before they join.

Value roleplay

Stay in character, react realistically and give other players room to build a scene.

No random violence

Conflict should have roleplay behind it. Random attacks and low-effort scenes damage the city.

Respect the community

Keep out-of-character conduct respectful and use staff channels when a situation needs review.

Protect progression

Do not exploit bugs, duplicate items or abuse systems. Report any issues through the official staff channels.

Build stories

The goal is not simply winning. Create situations that other players can respond to and remember.

Follow staff direction

Staff decisions, applications and detailed rules are handled through the official Discord.
