GLRP COMMUNITY STANDARD

City Rules.

Read the full detailed rules in Discord before playing. These core standards apply everywhere in the city.

01
◇

Respect the Community

Treat players and staff with respect. Harassment, discrimination, threats and targeted abuse are not accepted.

02
✦

Value Roleplay

Remain in character during active scenes, value your life and give other players a fair opportunity to respond.

03
△

Fair Combat

No random killing, vehicle deathmatching, combat logging, exploiting or returning without a valid roleplay reason.

04
⬡

Meaningful Crime

Criminal activity should create roleplay, not only rewards. Follow all robbery, gang and cooldown rules in Discord.

05
↯

Use Support Properly

Report problems through Discord tickets with clips or evidence. Never disrupt a live scene to argue rules.

06
⌁

Know the Rulebook

Joining the server means you agree to follow the complete current rulebook and staff directions published in Discord.
