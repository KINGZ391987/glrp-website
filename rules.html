GLRP COMMUNITY STANDARD

City Rules

These starter rules are ready for you to replace with your complete Discord rulebook before publishing.

CONTENTS 01 Respect 02 Roleplay 03 Combat 04 Crime 05 Support
01

Respect the Community

Treat players and staff with respect. Harassment, discrimination, threats and targeted abuse are not accepted.

02

Value Roleplay

Remain in character during active scenes, value your character’s life and give other players a fair opportunity to respond.

03

Fair Combat

No random killing, vehicle deathmatching, combat logging, exploiting or returning to a scene without a valid roleplay reason.

04

Meaningful Crime

Criminal activity must create roleplay rather than only rewards. Follow all robbery, gang and cooldown rules published in Discord.

05

Use Support Properly

Report issues through the correct Discord ticket. Provide clips or evidence where possible and never disrupt a live scene to argue rules.
